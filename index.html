Our Story

We are a specialized space design boutique that believes in creating spaces that transform the way you think, speak, act, work and live. We integrate soft human sciences, art, nature and technology to build humanistic, functional and creative spaces, be it a home or an office or a hotel! Our meticulous process to understand our clients, seamless design strategy and technically flawless & artistically impeccable execution makes our passion a delightful experience for our clients. It is not just designing spaces, it is designing a new life that you have always dreamt of! To learn more about our ways and how we can help you.

Meet the Team

Mark Jose

mark@bowerandpuffer.com

Mark is an artist by birth and designer by choice. His exposure to construction sites at an early age inspired him to passionately play with spaces and paved him way to a career in interior designing. Mark enjoys scheming bright colors with suave and light palettes creating dramatic visual appeals.

Olivia Walter

olivia@bowerandpuffer.com

Olivia is an Italian artist, visual storyteller and design enthusiast who has ventured into space designing. She believes in creating spaces that speak tales about the people who dwell in there and inspire them in their everyday lives. She loves combining nature, science, art, magic, mythology and technology in her work.
